Quick start

Upload an eye image on the AI Page and get an analysis back.

CCP Vision AI assists in detecting eye conditions using advanced deep-learning analysis. Use the sidebar to move between pages, and head to the AI Page whenever you're ready to run a scan.

  • The sidebar is always available on the left to jump between pages
  • The AI Page is where images are uploaded and analyzed
  • About and Developers have more background on the project

Getting started

What CCP Vision AI does

It assists in detecting eye conditions using advanced deep-learning analysis, giving you a fast, early read before you see a specialist.

Navigation

1

Use the sidebar to move between pages

The icons on the left stay visible everywhere — hover any icon for its label.

2

Access the AI Page to upload and analyze eye images

Select the bot icon in the sidebar to open the AI Page.

3

Open other pages for details, help, or information

About, Developers, and Data Security round out the rest of the app.

AI Page usage

1

Upload a clear eye image

Good lighting and a sharp, close-up photo of the eye give the most reliable read.

2

Let the system analyze it

The system will analyze the image and display the predicted condition in a few seconds.

This is a screening tool, not a diagnosis. Always confirm results with an eye care professional.
