About
Why we built this

Millions lose their sight to conditions that were entirely catchable.

CCP Vision AI is a privacy-first companion for deep-learning eye condition screening — built to give early, accurate detection to anyone, not just those with easy access to a specialist.

Mission

Secure, accessible screening — before symptoms become damage.

We provide secure and accessible AI-powered eye screening that enables early and accurate detection of ocular conditions, so a warning sign never has to wait for a waitlist.

Vision

A future where deep learning is part of everyday care.

We're building toward a future where deep-learning healthcare tools empower everyone with early detection and reliable medical insight — regardless of where they live or who they have access to.

The team

Built by student engineers across four disciplines.

CCP Vision AI was developed by a team specializing in the fields that make trustworthy medical AI possible.

Artificial Intelligence

Model architecture, training, and validation.

Cybersecurity

Keeping patient data and scans protected end to end.

Medical Imaging

Clinical grounding for how the model reads an eye.

Software Engineering

Turning the model into something anyone can use.
