Data Security

How CCP Vision AI protects your privacy, data, and confidentiality.

Zero Data Storage

CCP Vision AI never stores conversations, images, or logs. All data is cleared instantly after use.

Privacy-by-Design Architecture

Your data is never stored, shared, sold, or used to train any model.

End-to-End Encryption

All communication is encrypted during transit to prevent unauthorized access.

No Third-Party Tracking

No ads, analytics trackers, cookies, or fingerprinting scripts.
